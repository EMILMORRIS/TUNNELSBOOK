"ELUCID"

A story of primordial eternity.

Written by Emil W. R. Morris

Lexia is a nine year old girl when she falls into a coma after the doctors administer drugs to treat her recently developed severe PTSD. While in deep sleep, Lexia found herself in a parallel underworld where she inherits a body of a young Sage. The city ELUCID is under no rule except for the local born species who guard the knowledge of the world, the Gaaards. Everyone who lands there has the same objective, fight and kill the demons one fears most, get to the underworlds core and reconnect with their sleeping body. There are ancient tunnel systems, and some lead back out to the surface or a dead end. Once the line to the underworld is crossed, the first demon appears. They reemerge every twelve hours for each Sage, and one cannot kill another Sages demons. Cooperation is out of the rule except for a crystal that provides the ability to pass on fear once for the other Sage. The underworlds Core configures as the clock shifts every hour, and on the twelve hour the CORE freezes and demons are released. The underworld is divided into four layers reaching the Core, as the layers descend the demons become more sophisticated in their attack. They also increase by one every shift. There is only one problem, if the sage dies, they are sent back to ELUCID, but their demon count does not disappear. Some are stuck there for centuries.

Our hero, Lexia, is summoned to the underworld realm. She must fight the demons to reemerge with her sleeping body and appear back on earth, but something tells her that she might not wake up on Earth when she reaches the core.
